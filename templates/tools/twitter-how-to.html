How To Setup Twitter

Open Twitter for iPhone

From The Far Right Tab Select “Accounts & Settings”

Select “Settings”

Scroll To “Services” And Select It

Pick “Image Service”

Tap “Custom…”

Enter https://mltshp.com/upload

Tap “Save”

Start A New Tweet And Click The Character Count

Take A Photo

Post Your Tweet

The URL To The Photo Will Be Appended
